This collection of succulents, cacti, ground covers, and anything generally alien looking is currated by Liam English-Birge and Teya Sharir. The garden currently contains around 13 different species of succulents and cacti, and over 24 seperate plants. The garden is lit by 4 60w LEDs with color temperatures ranging from 2000k to 2700k and one 15w 18" fluorescent tube light. All lights are turned off and on after 6 hour intervals to reduce the risk of sunburning or stunting the plants.
