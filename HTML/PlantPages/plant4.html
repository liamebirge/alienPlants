Plant Description

  • Name: Zwartkop
  • Species Name: Abonium Arboreum
  • Care: This plant does not like really hot or dry weather. May go dormant during summer. Does not require water except in excessively dry conditions. In extreme heat, their leaves will curl to prevent excessive water loss.
    Growth season is winter to spring, when temperatures are cool (65â€“75f) and damp.
  • Flowering Period: bright yellow flowers in the spring
  • Propagation: This plant has the ability to produce roots along it's stems, which you may notice if the plant gets pot bound or the stems fall and touch the soil. The stem roots will quickly turn the fallen pieces into new plants.
