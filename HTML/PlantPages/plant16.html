Plant #16

Plant Description

  • Species Name: Echeveria Riviera
  • Care: Water only when soil is dry to the touch, does best in bright filtered light
  • Propogation: Cut thick, healthy leaves from mother plant. Lay leaves flat on a tray in a warm spot with indirect sun until their cut edges develop calluses. Uncallused cuttings rot in moist rooting medium. Callus formation takes between one day to two weeks. When fully callused, set the leaves upright in pot of medium ensuring full contact with medium. Tiny roots and leaves emerge from the bases of the withering leaves.
  • Flowering Period: Flowers at the same time every year
