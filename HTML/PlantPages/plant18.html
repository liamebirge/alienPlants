Plant #18

Plant Description

  • Name: Mandarin Orange Tree
  • Species Name: Citrus reticulata
  • Care: Keep in roomy pot filled with rich well-draining soil. Place in sunny location, grow lights as well as rotating the plant regularly can be used to provide light to unexposed areas. Plenty of water is important for Mandarin orange trees, however be careful not to over-water. Trees grow best when watered thoroughly and allowed to drain. Allow soil to dry out slightly between waterings. Maintain a steady temperature of 60-80°F(15-20°C). Protect plant from frost, so best choice would be to leave plant outside during spring, summer and fall, then bringing it in during winter
