Plant #19

Plant Description

  • Name: Medicinal Aloe
  • Species Name: Aloe Vera
  • Care: Plant in wide container filled with fast draining soil. Keep in full sunlight. Water every few days or when first 2 inches of soil is dry to the touch, whichever comes last. Water less frequently in winter months
