Plant #10

Plant Description

  • Species Name: Graptopetalum paraguayense
  • Care: Grow in dry sunny climate, watering only when needed
  • Propagation: Gently remove a fullgrown healthy leaf closest to the base of the plant by gently wiggling the leaf back and forth and twisting the entire leaf off. leave chosen leaves to dry for 2-4 days until the open stem has calloused. Place leaves onto seperate small planters filled with well draining soil. slightly press the stem into the soil so that the soil is barely covering the tip of the stem. Water every other day with a spray bottle, water only at the stem area as that is where the roots will emerge. Do not let the leaf soak in water or damp soil as it will rot
  • Flowering Period: Unknown
