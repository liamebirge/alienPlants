Plant #2

Plant Description

  • Species Name: Mammillaria carmenae
  • Care: Drought tolerant when established. Needs well draining soil. Water thoroughly when soil is dry to the touch. Protect from frost to prevent possible scarring. Looks best with regular (semi-daily) watering in hotter climates. Water once a month during winter and fall months and twice a month during summer and spring
  • Flowering Period: Spring
