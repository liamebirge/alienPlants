Plant #14

Plant Description

  • Name: Ruby Ball, Moon Cactus 'Hibotan'
  • Species Name: Gymnocalycium Mihanovichii Hibotan
  • Care: Needs semi frequent watering when soil becomes dry, being careful not to overwater.
    Keep in dimmer diffused light.
    Keep warmer in summer months and colder in winter months.
  • Propagation: As the cactus ages, it will start to develop knobules, as these grow larger (about the size of a dime) gently cut or wiggle them from the parent plant removing the entire plantling. Place this cutting in moist cactus soil with the open cut facing towards the soil
