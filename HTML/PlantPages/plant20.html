Plant #20

Plant Description

  • Name: Pinwheel
  • Species Name: Aeonium Haworthii
  • Care: Grow in moist shade as this plant does not like excessively hot or dry weather. Grow most in spring when temperatures are cooler (65–75°F / 18–24°C). Too much moisture will make the roots rot
