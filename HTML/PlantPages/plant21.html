Plant #21

Plant Description

  • Species Name: Graptopetalum pentandrum ssp. superbum
  • Care: Give plant bright indirect sunlight. Water sparingly, no more than twice a month. Grow in fast draining medium, gravel or 2 parts dirt to 1 part perelite works very well.
