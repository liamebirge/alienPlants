New Plant #4

Plant Description

  • Name: Sedevaria 'Blue Elf' Hybrid
  • Species Name: Assorted Species
  • Care: This plant is a hybrid between the Sedum and Echevaria genuses, so the leaves are easily knocked off of the plant. Likes well draining soil and prefers more waterings during summer months and less during winter (let soil dry out completely between waterings), keep warmer in summer months and colder in winter months.
