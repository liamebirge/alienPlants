This website is a photographic and textual record of all the succulents, cacti, and any generally alien looking plant that passes through my hands. This garden is in USDA plant hardiness zone 7b to 9a, and since outside temperatures fluctuate unpredictably due to coastal weather the garden is kept indoors for a lot of the year.
